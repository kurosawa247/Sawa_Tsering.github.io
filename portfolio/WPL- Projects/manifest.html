Evidence title: WPL: Projects

Evidence description:

At CERcuits, I worked on several concrete infrastructure projects in a structured and project-based way. For each task, I first discussed the planning with my mentor, Jef, so we could agree on how and when to carry out the work with as little disturbance as possible. This was especially important when making changes to the network, replacing hardware, or planning backup and migration work that could affect ongoing operations.

I contributed to these projects by carrying out the technical tasks myself and following the agreed approach step by step. Since I worked mainly with my mentor rather than in a larger group, my contribution focused on carrying out the planned work independently, reporting progress, and adjusting the execution when needed. This helped me understand how a real infrastructure project is planned, coordinated, and completed in a company environment.

What I learned

  • Planning tasks before starting the work.

  • Making agreements on when and how to perform changes.

  • Minimizing disruption during infrastructure changes.

  • Working in a structured way with my mentor.

  • Following a project from preparation to completion.
