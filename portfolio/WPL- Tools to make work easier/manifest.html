Evidence title: WPL: Tools to make work easier

Evidence description:

During my internship, I used several tools to work more efficiently and keep my tasks well organized. I used NetBox to document the network structure, devices, interfaces, and connections in a clear and structured way. I also used WinSCP to transfer files safely between systems, which helped me manage configuration files and other important documents more easily. In Confluence, I recorded agreements, naming conventions, and documentation so that the information stayed available and up to date for future use.

By using these tools consistently, I was able to work in a more structured way and reduce mistakes. They helped me keep track of changes, store information centrally, and make my work easier to understand for both myself and others. Even though I did not automate everything with scripts, I did use tools to make my work more efficient, organized, and professional.
