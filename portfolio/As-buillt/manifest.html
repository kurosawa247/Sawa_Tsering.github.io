Evidence title: As-buillt

Evidence description:

For my IT Project class, we approached the WiFi survey project in a structured and project-based way. We first defined the scope of the project, divided the tasks between my project partner and me, and planned how we would collect and document the required information. After that, we carried out the survey step by step, gathered measurements, and compared our results to make sure our documentation stayed consistent.

During the project, we also had to adjust our approach when we encountered new information or practical issues. This meant that we regularly reviewed our progress, updated our documentation, and coordinated with each other to keep the project on track. In the end, the project resulted in a complete and well-organized report of the wireless environment.

What I learned

  • How to work according to a clear project structure.

  • How to divide tasks and responsibilities with a teammate.

  • How to plan and follow up on project progress.

  • How to adapt the project approach when issues occur.

  • How to keep documentation aligned with the project goals.

  • How to deliver a complete final result.
