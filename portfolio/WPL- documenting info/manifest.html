Evidence title: WPL: documenting info.

Evidence description:

When I started my internship at CERcuits, there was almost no inventory or clear information available about the infrastructure. This made it difficult to work efficiently, and even some employees were not fully aware of how the network was structured. That is why I began documenting everything in NetBox and Confluence.

Because of this documentation, the network structure became much clearer and it is now much easier to work with the infrastructure in the future. Information can be found faster, is better organized, and is more useful for everyone who will work with the network later.
