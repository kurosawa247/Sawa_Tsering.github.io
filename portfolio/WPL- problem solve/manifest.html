Evidence title: WPL: problem solve

Evidence description:

During my internship at CERcuits, I investigated and solved several hardware-related problems on both the servers and the backup servers. The main focus was finding the cause of performance issues and looking for the right solution. For example, we replaced RAM, added SSDs, and upgraded the CPU to a more powerful model to make the systems faster and more stable.

These changes were necessary to ensure that backups and restores could be completed on time without the systems becoming too slow. By carefully analyzing these problems and solving them step by step, I learned the importance of looking beyond the symptoms and identifying the underlying hardware cause.
